OLD STRUCTURE (Cumulative):
- Period 1: Full scenario
- Period 2: Full scenario + all events/actions from Period 1
- Period 3: Full scenario + all events/actions from Periods 1-2
- Period 10: Full scenario + all events/actions from Periods 1-9 (VERY LONG)

NEW STRUCTURE (Incremental with Context):
- Period 1: Full detailed scenario with rich backstory
- Periods 2-10: Brief scenario context + changes since last period + current
  situation + recent events/actions (ONLY from previous period)

NEW PERIOD STRUCTURE (PERIODS 2-10)

1. SCENARIO CONTEXT (Constant 2-3 Sentence Reminder)
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────
   "The People's Federation of Novaris launched a pressure campaign against
   The Democratic Commonwealth of Tethys, citing historical territorial claims.
   Tethys has mounted a determined defense with international support."

   → Same text for all periods 2-10
   → Just enough to ground someone who returns after a break

2. DEVELOPMENTS SINCE LAST PERIOD (Days X-Y)
   ──────────────────────────────────────────
   **Key Changes:**
     • Military balance: Shifted from "..." to "..."
     • Crisis intensity: Moved from X/10 to Y/10
     • International sanctions: Adjusted from X% to Y% implementation
     • International support: Moved from X% to Y%

   → Shows ONLY changes from previous period to current period
   → Allows participants to track trajectory
   → Only lists metrics that actually changed (omits stable indicators)

3. CURRENT SITUATION — DAY X
   ─────────────────────────
   **MILITARY SITUATION**
   [Natural language description of current state]

   **ECONOMIC PRESSURE**
   [Natural language description of sanctions]

   **DIPLOMATIC SITUATION**
   [Natural language description of support]

   → Current state as of this period
   → Natural language instead of raw numbers

4. RECENT EVENTS AND ACTIONS
   ─────────────────────────
   **MAJOR EVENTS**
   [Events from previous period only]

   **REPORTED ACTIONS**
   [Actions from previous period only]

   → Shows ONLY what happened in the period just completed
   → No cumulative history

BENEFITS OF NEW STRUCTURE
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

✅ MANAGEABLE LENGTH
   • Period 1: ~160 lines (full rich scenario)
   • Period 2: ~100 lines (context + update)
   • Period 10: ~100 lines (stays consistent, not cumulative)

   OLD: Period 10 would have been 500+ lines
   NEW: Period 10 stays around 100 lines

✅ CLEAR TRAJECTORY
   • "Developments Since Last Period" shows direction of change
   • Participants can see trends without being overwhelmed
   • Easy to compare t-1 to t

✅ REDUCED COGNITIVE LOAD
   • Brief context provides grounding
   • Focus on "what's new" rather than re-reading everything
   • Mimics how real intelligence briefings work

✅ NO DETAILED NOTE-TAKING REQUIRED
   • Participants don't need to track everything manually
   • Changes section provides enough context
   • Can complete periods across multiple sessions

✅ REALISTIC INFORMATION FLOW
   • Professional analysts work with "what changed" briefings
   • News reports focus on recent developments
   • More authentic forecasting experience
