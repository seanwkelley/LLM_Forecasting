## Part 2: Deep Decision-Making Pattern Analysis

### PRESIDENTIAL COUNTER-PROPOSAL PATTERNS

**Total Counter-Proposals:** 7 instances across 5 periods

#### Pattern 1: Risk Mitigation (Most Common)
Decision makers consistently counter-propose LESS risky alternatives:

**Example 1 - Tethys (Liberal Institutionalist):**
- COUNTER: intelligence_gathering (was: cyber_attack)
- Rationale: "Enhanced surveillance of logistics is a lawful activity that supports our defense, versus escalatory cyber attacks that violate norms"

**Example 2 - Novaris (Realist):**
- COUNTER: troop_movements (was: border_incursion)
- Rationale: "Border incursion is premature and risks full-scale escalation; repositioning achieves same pressure with deniability"

**Example 3 - Tethys (Liberal Institutionalist):**

#### Pattern 2: Strategic Sophistication Over Symbolism

**Example - Novaris (Realist):**

**Insight:** Decision makers prefer *substance over symbolism*

### DECISION MAKER QUALITY INDICATORS

#### 1. Multi-Factor Reasoning
Decision makers don't make single-dimension choices:

**Complex approval example:**
"A show of force is necessary to project strength [domestic politics] and maintain morale [psychological] without crossing a clear line of aggression [escalation management]"

**Complex veto example:**
"Cyber attacks violate norms [legal], could invite retaliation we're ill-prepared to absorb [capability assessment], and are escalatory [strategic risk]"

#### 2. Sequential Logic Recognition
Decision makers catch redundancies:

"The primary recommendation already achieves forward positioning; no need for duplicate expenditure" (VETO secondary troop movement after approving primary)

#### 3. Long-term Strategic Thinking

**Novaris Strategic Stockpiling:**
COUNTER: strategic_stockpiling (was: financial_aid)
Rationale: "Instead of subsidies, stockpile surplus production; supports producers, builds reserves, denies surplus to market, increases long-term leverage"

This shows *4-dimensional chess*: economic, strategic, market manipulation, long-term planning.
