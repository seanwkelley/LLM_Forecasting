ENGAGEMENT TECHNIQUES USED:

1. IMMERSIVE FRAMING
   "Imagine you're an analyst at a leading think tank. A major geopolitical
   crisis is unfolding in real-time."
   → Immediately puts participant in the role

2. STAKES AND TENSION
   "The clock is ticking. Tethys's government hangs in the balance."
   → Creates urgency and importance

3. REAL-WORLD CONNECTIONS
   "Think of this like forecasting whether Ukraine's government would collapse
   in 2022, or whether the Afghan government would fall in 2021."
   → Makes it concrete and relatable

4. ACCESSIBLE EXPERTISE
   "Like professional forecasters at organizations such as Good Judgment Project
   or Metaculus..."
   → Connects to respected institutions

5. VIVID SPECIFICS
   "Novaris has mobilized 40% of its armed forces...control over critical
   energy pipelines that Novaris depends on for 30% of its export revenue."
   → Concrete details make it real

6. CONVERSATIONAL CLARITY
   "Here's the catch: You won't have perfect information."
   → Direct, conversational style maintains engagement

7. NARRATIVE STRUCTURE
   Each section tells a story with clear beginning, middle, and end
   → Helps participants follow along
